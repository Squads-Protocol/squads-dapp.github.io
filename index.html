squads

What are Squads

Squads is user-friendly DAO generator on Solana, which combines a treasury, on-chain voting and a chat all in one interface

who is Squads for?

Protocol and NFT DAOs

DeFi protocols and NFT communities can register their token with Squads to allow their holders to participate in governance via proposals and on-chain voting

who is Squads for?

Startup teams, investors and freelancers

Joining a hackathon with your team, looking to co-invest with friends or doing development work for a bounty? Just spin up a Squad in a few clicks to run your internal governance or just use Squads as a multisig

who is Squads for?

Gaming guilds and communities

Looking to conquer Star Atlas or Aurory with your guild? Create a Squad to hold in-game assets jointly and manage your guild via on-chain proposals
